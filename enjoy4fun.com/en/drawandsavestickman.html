About Draw And Save Stickman

Want to test your IQ, creativity or drawing skills? Looking for a new, unique puzzle game? Draw and Save Stickman is the game you need! Make drawings to keep various dangers and traps away from Stickman. You will need to think differently in each level. Let's guess where the danger will come from and start drawing the shield!

How to play Draw And Save Stickman online?

Create a protection shield by making drawings using "MOUSE"
