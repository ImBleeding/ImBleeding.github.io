About Hero Rescue

Hero Rescue is a skill game developed by QKY Games. In this game, you are in charge of controlling a rescue helicopter. Click or tap on the screen to lift the rescue chopper up. Pilot the chopper skillfully and rescue civilians from a wildfire. You get more stars if you don't leave anyone behind. Can you finish Hero Rescue without any casualties?

How to play Hero Rescue online?
