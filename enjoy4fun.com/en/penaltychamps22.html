About penalty champs 22

It's time for another Penalty Shootout World Cup. Choose your national team, shoot and defend penalties, and reach the trophy. You can play the championship with or without the group stage. In addition to the classic game mode, you can also play against your friends on WiFi.

How to play penalty champs 22 online?

When shooting, click three times to determine the direction, height and strength of the shot. When defending, it is enough to click once, on the place where you want the goalkeeper to dive (immediately before the shot you will see a target that shows where the opponent will shoot).
