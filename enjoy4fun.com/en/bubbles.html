About Bubbles

Bubbles is a bubble-shooter puzzle game in which your goal is to pop all the bubbles from your screen. You can do so by aiming to group at least three instances of the same colored bubbles. Try and make large chains to increase your points as well as the overall fun! How high can you score in Bubbles?
