Merge the Numbers

About Merge the Numbers

Merge the Numbers is a skill and puzzle game created by Eagle Games. In this addictive multiplying experience, you have to merge blocks that have the same numbers to increase their value. Keep merging to get the highest score possible! But be careful, if you can't merge, there will be a new row of blocks that appear. If the screen is filled, it's game over for you! How high can you go in Merge the Numbers?

How to play Merge the Numbers online?

Drag a tile and drop it on an identical tile. They will merge and multiply in value. Keep going until you can reach the highest number.
