About Super Rainbow Friends

Super Rainbow Friends is a fast-paced action game where you'll have to run away from enemies in order to survive until the end. In this adventure, you will compete against other users in all kinds of mini-games where the goal will always be the same: avoid being killed and defeat anyone who approaches you. Compete against other players to be the best racer and collect stars to unlock new characters

How to play Super Rainbow Friends online?

PC controls: Use the mouse one click to jump and two to double jump Mobile and Tablet controls: Tap the screen to jump and double tap to double jump
