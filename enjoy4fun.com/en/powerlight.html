Power Light

About Power Light

Power Light is a thinking game developed by QKY Games. In this game, you'll fix all unconnected wires which can transfer power to the bulb from the battery and complete all the puzzles to have fun. This game is much more fun when you share the game with your friends and compare your scores!

How to play Power Light online?

Tap or click on the tiles to rotate them and connect the power sources.
