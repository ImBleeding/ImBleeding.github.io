Bucket Crusher

About Bucket Crusher

Bucket Crusher is a satisfying casual game where you tear down walls brick by brick using a circular saw. The bricks go into your Bucket Crusher and get converted into cash for various upgrades!

How to play Bucket Crusher online?

Cut through walls Cut through the wall and push the bricks into your Bucket Crusher to convert them into coin. You can do this until your fuel runs out. Upgrade your machinery After each run, you can use the money you earned from crushing bricks to upgrade your machinery. Increase the length, power, size, and fuel capacity to get more bricks with every run of the level. Tear down brickwork mosaics Eventually, you’ll have enough upgrades to tear down the entire wall until no bricks remain untouched. Once you complete the first wall, there are various colorful and interesting mosaics with different shapes to dig into and pull to pieces next time! You also earn new skins as you progress.
