About 2048 x2 Merge Blocks

A combination of the classic game of 2048 and Tetris. The numbers are dropping from above and keep coming. Manage to merge numbers and keep the playing field clean for as long as possible. What is the best merge you can make?

How to play 2048 x2 Merge Blocks online?

Tap on the desired columns to drop the falling numbers. Connect identical numbers to merge them into higher numbers. You can also combine more than two numbers for higher multiples.
