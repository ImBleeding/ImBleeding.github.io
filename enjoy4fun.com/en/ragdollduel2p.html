About Ragdoll Duel 2p

Ragdoll Duel is a funny duel game that can be played by 1 and 2 player. Shoot wisely in this ragdoll game, time your shots to aim and shoot down your opponent. You can increase your health bar and weapon power with the coins you earn in 1 Player mode.

How to play Ragdoll Duel 2p online?

Game Controls; Player 1: "W" Player 2: "UP ARROW" Also, you can use "MOUSE" for 1 Player!
