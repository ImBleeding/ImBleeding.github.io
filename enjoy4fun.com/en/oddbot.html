About Odd Bot

Help a tiny robot stumble through puzzling worlds. Tap to walk, swipe to turn, and drag elevators to reach the golden cube!

How to play Odd Bot online?

Tap on the world to walk, drag the tiny white squares block to move sliders and elevators, and swipe anywhere else to rotate the view of the camera. Get to the golden cube to win!
