About Stick War New Age

A new one has been added to stickman games. This is Stick War: New Age. The stick fighting game opens to you the doors of an exciting adventure world. What are you waiting for to join? Click and join the world of stickman stick fighting. Take care of your Stickman character. Your opponents are waiting for you for the Stick fight. With the Armed Stickman, you can also destroy your opponents and some of the obstacles that come across you. Are you ready to become a stick fighter? Many obstacles are waiting for you in the game. Swinging barbed balls, wheels with rotating blades, moving rocks. In each chapter you will encounter obstacles that are more difficult than others.You have to master them all and finish all 40 episodes successfully. Proceed step by step to victory. Enjoy the most exciting stickman stick fighting game of adventure games. You can play the game anywhere you want. You can play Stick fight the game with your friends at school or at home. Hours of fun and excitement are waiting for you.
