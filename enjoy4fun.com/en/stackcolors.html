About Stack Colors

Stack Colors is a casual game where you must move and stack the right color objects and finally kick them in the finish line to get a huge score boost in a satisfying way! Avoid objects of other colors, since picking them up would lead to you losing objects you already collect. Once you lose all of the objects you already get, it's game over! There are also places where the color can change rapidly and you will have to react quickly to pick up the correct object. This is a simple yet challenging game!

How to play Stack Colors online?

Drag the left mouse button to move the character to the left or right.
