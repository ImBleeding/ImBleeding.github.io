About Santa Parkour

Santa Parkour is a casual game where you play as a Santa to deliver presents by parkour. Jump on snowy rooftops while avoiding obstacles in this exciting fresh take on parkour. Feel the rush of flying through the air, then execute sick rolls as you land. Unlock different kinds of moves by collecting skill cards. Do different types of flips as a Santa!

How to play Santa Parkour online?

Press the left mouse button to jump.
