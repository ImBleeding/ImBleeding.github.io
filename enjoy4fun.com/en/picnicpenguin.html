About Picnic Penguin

Picnic Penguin is a puzzle game created by Neutronized. Help the penguin to push tasty treats onto the picnic blanket by pushing multiple blocks at a time and rearranging them to open the path. There are several worlds to unlock with a huge variation of puzzles to solve while finding coins to collect all the skins on the gashapon machine. Are you ready for a sick picnic?

How to play Picnic Penguin online?

Push multiple blocks at a time and rearrange them to open the path and complete the levels.
