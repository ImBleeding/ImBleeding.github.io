About Endless Truck

Go on an endless truck ride in this fun driving game! Complete missions at each level. Collect money to buy upgrades. You can earn extra money by performing cool stunts. If you get a perfect landing you get a huge speed boost so aim for those as well. Keep going as long as you don't run out of fuel!

How to play Endless Truck online?

Press space bar to jump Press left or right arrow to flip
