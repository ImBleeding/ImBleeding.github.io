About 4 Colors Tour Multiplayer

Pack your bags and get ready to travel around the world with a bunch of cute mascots in this special version of Four Colors! Match cards by color or number, play action cards to mix the game up and be the first to get rid of all cards. Last but not least: Do not forget to press the 1 button when you have only one card left! 2 Game’s Mode: - Multiplayer mode - Play against the pc

How to play 4 Colors Tour Multiplayer online?

Multiplayer Collect coins to unlock new skins. Match cards by color or number and get rid of them first. Don't forget to say "Uno" when you only have one card left, otherwise you will have to draw two penalty cards.
