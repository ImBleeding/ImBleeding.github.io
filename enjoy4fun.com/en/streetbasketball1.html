About Street Basketball 1

Select your favourite player and show your free throws skills! You have one minute and 25 shots to get as many balls as possible into the basket! Striped balls are worth 2 points (instead of 1): concentrate on these special balls for extra points!

How to play Street Basketball 1 online?

Mobile: Tap the screen to stop the indicator and throw the ball PC: Press spacebar or mouse button to stop the indicator and throw the ball
