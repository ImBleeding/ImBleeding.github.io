About Twirl Pja

Twirl Pja is a puzzle game where you need to fulfill your goals by placing blocks on the board until they get full horizontally or vertically to make the blocks disappear. The more you show your skills, the more stars you get for completing the level. Throughout the game, you will meet various levels that can captivate and challenge any player.

How to play Twirl Pja online?

Drag and release the left mouse button to place the block.
