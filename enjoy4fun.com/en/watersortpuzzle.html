Water Sort Puzzle

About Water Sort Puzzle

Water Sort Puzzle is a fun and addictive puzzle game! Try to sort the colored water in the glasses until all colors in the same glass. A challenging yet relaxing game to exercise your brain!

How to play Water Sort Puzzle online?

HOW TO PLAY: Tap any glass to pour water to another glass. The rule is that you can only pour the water if it is linked to the same color and there’re enough space on the glass. Try not to get stuck - but don’t worry, you can always restart the level at any time.
