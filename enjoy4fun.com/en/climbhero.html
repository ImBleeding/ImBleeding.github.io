Climb Hero

About Climb Hero

He trained and gained strength for years and now he wants to show everyone that he is the climb hero. Help him choose the right stones and climb together to the very top of the mountain. Can you do it with one try?

How to play Climb Hero online?

It's very simple. The hero is hanging on a stone and your task is to tap at the right moment to make him hang on another stone. Along the way, you collect diamonds for which you can customize your character.
