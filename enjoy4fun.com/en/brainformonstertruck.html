About Brain For Monster Truck

Brain For Monster Truck is a monster truck game where have to make your own path to get success! Try to collect all the stars by drawing the right track and make sure to keep your cargo on board!

How to play Brain For Monster Truck online?

Use mouse swipe to create roads and left and right keys to move.
