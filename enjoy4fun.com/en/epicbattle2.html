About Stickman Fighter: Epic Battle 2

Stickman Fighter: Epic Battle 2 is a stickman fighting game where you an unleash your anger at waves of enemy stickmen. This game is a level-based game where your enemies get stronger and stronger. The better you fight however, the stronger you become because you can buy and upgrade weapons that you will receive in-game. Be sure to have the correct timing. Fight it out in the arena to become the victorious stickman. Play Stickman Fighter: Epic Battle 2 for free on GameBridge in your browser on desktop or on mobile.
