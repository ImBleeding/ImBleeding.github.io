About Blondie Reload

Look who's back! This is the charming Blonde and her boyfriend. They are a great stylish couple. Just take a look at their wonderful outfits inspired by the fashion of the 80s. Charge yourself with the radiant energy of Blondie and her boyfriend, plunge into the world of high fashion with your lovers on the sunny coast and among the lights of the night city. Dance, have fun and roller-skate through the streets of the metropolis or along the promenade.

How to play Blondie Reload online?

Help Blondie and her boyfriend to plunge into the atmosphere of the bright 80s. Create a suitable makeup; choose stylish outfits and accessories. Save the resulting result as a PNG-image. Depending on the gaming device, a computer mouse click or a simple touch on touch screens is used for control.
