About Dunkers Fight 2P

Get ready for basketball fun with the Dunkers Fight 2P game. Get ready when the countdown starts and grab the ball before your opponent. Try to throw your ball and hit the pot. If your opponent grabs the ball from your hand, resist him and protect the pot. Funny 2-person game your time playing with friends at school will pass with pleasure. Enjoy!

How to play Dunkers Fight 2P online?

Player 1 Move: A-D Keys Player 1 Shoot: W-S Keys Player 2 Move: Left-Right Arrow Keys player 2 Shoot: Up-Down Arrow keys
