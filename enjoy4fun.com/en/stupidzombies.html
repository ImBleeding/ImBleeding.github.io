About stupid-zombies

Stupid Zombies is a puzzle zombie shooting game developed by MarketJS. In this game, you’ll fight against zombies by aiming correctly with your gun and shooting bullets with rebounds. Work on your angles to score the highest number of zombie kills in the least amount of bullets. Go ahead and demonstrate the power of your aim !
