About Slalom Hero

Slalom Hero is an arcade game developed by b10b. In this game, you can choose your hero and compete across three courses in the Winter Sports Slalom Event. Steer between the slalom gates, collect boosts to improve your times, and most importantly, have fun! Can you win a gold medal in Slalom Hero?
