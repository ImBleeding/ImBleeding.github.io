About Super Jumper

One of the hottest mobile games of all time! Easy to pick up and play. Journey up a sheet of graph paper, perpetually jumping from one platform to the next, avoiding black holes, and blasting baddies with nose balls along the way. Laugh with delight as you blow past other players' actual score markers scribbled in the margins. And be warned: this game is insanely addictive!
