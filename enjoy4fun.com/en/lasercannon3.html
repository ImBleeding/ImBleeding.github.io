About Laser Cannon 3

The new game in “Laser Cannon” series. New locations, new monsters, new gear. Kill’em all! Laser Cannon 3 is finally back and now the funny physics-based shooting game goes into the third round. It's up to you to save our planet one more time against a bunch of annoying alien creatures. Clear each stage using your mighty cannon and try to shoot them all with as less shots as possible.

How to play Laser Cannon 3 online?

The fewer shots you need, the better. So aim well before you fire and make sure that every shot is a hit. These strange creatures must be destroyed, and you are the only person who can do it. Are you ready for this responsible task? Find out now and have fun with Laser Cannon 3! Controls: Mouse = Aim / Shoot
