About HappyGlass

The glass is not happy because it is empty. Your task is to draw a line to fill the glass with liquid and make it laugh again! Try to find the best way to complete each level. You can come up with your own solution, so don't be afraid to think outside the box and get creative! Some levels may look easy, but let's see if you can actually get 3 stars. Features * Dynamic mechanism. Freely draw lines to complete levels! * Simple smart fun and challenging puzzle game * Lots of levels and more coming soon! * Fun and relaxing themes to keep you immersed

How to play HappyGlass online?

Swipe the screen to draw lines
