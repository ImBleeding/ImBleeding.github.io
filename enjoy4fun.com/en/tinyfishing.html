About Tiny Fishing

Tiny Fishing is a fishing game where you have to upgrade your gear to get bigger fish! Each fish you catch will earn you some money, which you can then spend on better equipment to fish deeper and deeper. The deeper you go, the more rare the fish become. Can you catch the biggest fish?

How to play Tiny Fishing online?

Click with your mouse to throw your line out. Hold and move around to catch different fish.
