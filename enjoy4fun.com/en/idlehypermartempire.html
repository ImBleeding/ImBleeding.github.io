About IdleHypermartEmpire

Idle Hypermart Empire is an idle simulation game where your workers start producing goods to sell. You can transport the profits to the cash counter office using the elevator and finally to your account. Use the earnings to upgrade your Hypermart, elevator, and warehouse. Automate tasks by hiring managers at each facility to save time and boost your earnings.

How to play IdleHypermartEmpire online?

Use the left mouse button to interact with the workers and in-game UI.
