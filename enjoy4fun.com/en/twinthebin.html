About Twin The Bin

Twin the Bin is a HTML5 Arcade Game. Have fun and learn to recycle with this educational (but absolutely not boring!) game! Collect as many objects as possible before the time runs out, but be careful! You must pick only the trash suited to the carried bin.

How to play Twin The Bin online?

Desktop: Use arrow keys to move your character Mobile: Use arrows to move your character
