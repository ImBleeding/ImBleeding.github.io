About Jewel Treasure

Jewel Treasure is an addictive and exciting adventure match 3 game filled with beautiful jewels! This jewel game has well designed puzzles for you to play and enjoy. Be the Jewel Master and unlock all levels!.

How to play Jewel Treasure online?

Match gems of the same type to eliminate them. Reach the target points of each level to move on.
