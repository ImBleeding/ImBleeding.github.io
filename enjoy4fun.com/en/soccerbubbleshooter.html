About Soccer Bubble Shooter

Aim, match and smash all the colorful soccer bubbles in this relaxing color matching adventure. To break the bubbles, you need to connect three or more bubbles of the same color together and then shoot the bubble you are given into a bunch of soccer bubbles stacked in the play area. Try to break all the bubbles with as few shots as possible. Play now and raise your name on the global leaderboard!

How to play Soccer Bubble Shooter online?

Mobile: - Tap and drag to change direction - Release to shoot Desktop: - Move pointer to change direction - Click to shoot
