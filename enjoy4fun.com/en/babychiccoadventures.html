About Baby Chicco Adventures

Baby Chicco Adventures is a platform game in which you'll help our little penguin escape the dangers of the video game world. Have fun playing and completing all the challenges in this fantastic game!

How to play Baby Chicco Adventures online?

A or left arrow key = move left . D or right arrow key = move right . W or Z or up arrow key = jump . X or J = shoot
