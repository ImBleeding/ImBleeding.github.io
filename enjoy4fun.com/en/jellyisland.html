Jelly Island

About Jelly Island

Pack your bags and set off to explore Jelly Island. A bunch of cute jellies can't wait to be collected by you! Discover the two game modes: -Time Attack: endless mode in which you have to fight against time -History: 25 levels of increasing difficulty with objectives to achieve, special jellies and obstacles to overcome

How to play Jelly Island online?

Swipe with your mouse or finger to move the pieces on board. Match 3 or more identical syimbols to make them disappear. In "Time Attack" mode the aim is to collect as many jellies as possible in the available time. In "Story" Mode you have to reach the level's goal in order to pass all the 25 levels available.
