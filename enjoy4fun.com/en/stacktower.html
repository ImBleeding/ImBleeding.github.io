About Stack Tower

Stack Tower is a fun game for the family. Build tall towers flawlessly without getting them ruined. By stacking shapes with different size on each other, ending up with a perfect building to complete each level.

How to play Stack Tower online?

Mouse to aim, click to drop the ruins. Touch and drag to aim and release to drop the ruins.
