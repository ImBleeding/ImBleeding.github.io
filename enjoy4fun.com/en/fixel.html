About Fixel

Oh no! I broke all the pixel art. Can you help fix it? Drag the pieces onto the pegboard and make sure the colors match!

How to play Fixel online?

Drag and drop pieces to the pegboard such that the correct colors are filled in. Additional controls: you can use two fingers to zoom in and out, and pan the whole level.
