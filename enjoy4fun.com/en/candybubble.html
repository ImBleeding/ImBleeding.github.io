About Candy Bubble

Candy Bubble is a fun lollipop-based bubble shooter game. Don't be fooled though; the game is as challenging as it is colorful! Have fun and try to beat all the levels.

How to play Candy Bubble online?

Move your mouse to aim and use the left mouse button to shoot. Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to make them disappear. Bounce balls against the walls to reach difficult areas.
