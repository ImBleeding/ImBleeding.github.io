About Cubes 2048.io

Cubes 2048.io is an addictive fusion game between Snake and 2048. Get a bigger number by collecting free cubes and eating other players with a smaller number than you. Your cubes with the same value that bump into one another will merge. You can also eat cubes with a smaller number than you, even if it is part of a bigger opponent. Stay careful since opponents can also eat your smaller cubes! Can you reach the biggest number in the arena?

How to play Cubes 2048.io online?

PC: Move mouse = change direction Hold left-click = speed up Mobile: Drag = move Double tap = speed up
