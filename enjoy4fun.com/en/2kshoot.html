About 2K Shoot

2K Shoot is an addictive combination of the 2048 Puzzle and bubble shooter game with merging gameplay as a surprise. Play Bubble 2K Shoot 2048 Ball: Shoot & Merge Puzzle and relax your brain! 2K Shoot & Merge Puzzle is a new puzzle to train your brain in such a funny way. Unlike other number merge puzzle games, the 2K Shoot & Merge Puzzle allows you to shoot balls as a bubble shooter, aim and shoot to match 2 same colored balls to merge into a double number ball!

How to play 2K Shoot online?

- Tap and hold the screen to aim a number of balls. - Shoot and match 2 same-colored balls. - Don't forget to use magical ball or bomb ball when you get stuck
