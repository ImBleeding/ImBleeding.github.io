Color Switch

About Color Switch

Tap to get the ball through the corresponding color!!

How to play Color Switch online?

A player controls a ball, initially blue. The objective of the game is to pass through obstacles, by going through the same color as the ball has, collecting coins in the process. Occasionally a diamond with 4 colors will be collected, changing the color of the ball to a random one. The ball jumps upward when the screen is tapped. The game ends once the ball has no more lives, or the ball falls to the bottom of the screen.
