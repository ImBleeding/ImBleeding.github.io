About Ball Maze

Balls Maze is a classic puzzle game, perfect for taking a quick break to train your brain. It’s designed to be a surprisingly simple solo game played by swiping through fun 2D retro labyrinths and popping balloons. Smash all the balloons in the maze and you will win!!!
