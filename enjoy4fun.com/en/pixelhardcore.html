About Pixel Hardcore

Pixel Journey is a platformer game. To complete the level, you need to find the key, and then head to the door. It seems simple, but various obstacles will be waiting for you on the way. Will you be able to overcome them all?

How to play Pixel Hardcore online?

Control is available on a computer and a mobile device . On a computer, use the WASD keys to control the character, on a mobile device, use the on-screen buttons.
