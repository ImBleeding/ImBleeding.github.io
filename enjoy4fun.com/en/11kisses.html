11 Kisses

About 11 Kisses

Angel-Girl and Demon-Boy were separated by fate. Help the Angel get to her lover and disenchant him with a kiss. You will have to solve small puzzles to make your way through different terrain. Use the bridges and ladders, and blow up all the bombs that block the passage. Good luck!

How to play 11 Kisses online?

Help two lovers overcome all difficulties and find each other. To do this, it is necessary to restore the road along, which the girl will be able to reach her lover. Depending on the gaming device, a computer mouse or touch on touch screens is used for control. Some objects need to be dragged from one place to another: click on the object and drag it to a new location.
