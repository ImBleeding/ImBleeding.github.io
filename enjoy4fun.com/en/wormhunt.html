About Worm Hunt

Worm.Hunt is a multiplayer game with the updated version of the old classic colored snake game - Worm Battle Royale! Collect as much food as possible and become the biggest snake in the arena! Many other worms will get in the way of your battle royale, but you can defeat them by upgrading your worm. Earn gold coins and gems and get unique skins for worms. Improve your skills, collect bonuses and participate in themed and secret events with unique gifts and tasks.

How to play Worm Hunt online?

Move the mouse to control the worm's direction Press the left mouse button, right mouse button, or space to speed up
