About cannon-strike

Cannon Strike is a skill game created by QKY Games. Your objective is to fill the bucket below with the colorful balls in your arsenal. Aim smart, shoot the cannon and fill all the buckets with colors. The balls you can't get into the bucket will deduct points from your final score. Go ahead and share Cannon Strike with your friends to compare your high scores!

How to play cannon-strike online?

Click or tap on the screen to shoot balls into the container below. Time your moves and try not to waste any balls.
