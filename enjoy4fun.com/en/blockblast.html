About Block Blast

Block Blast is a puzzle game developed by QKY Games. Your objective is to fill the empty tiles with the different types of blocks you see on the bottom of the screen. Simply drag the tile and drop it onto the empty slot. There are 50 levels to complete in this game. Can you finish them all before your friends? Share the game with them to find out!

How to play Block Blast online?

Fill the empty tiles by dragging and dropping a group of blocks that correspond to them.
