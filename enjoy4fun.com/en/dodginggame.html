About Dodging Game

Dodge obgects, grow a tail. Anyone got a 1UP?

How to play Dodging Game online?

Touch the screen and move left or right to adjust the yellow player's horizontal position. Avoid falling objects. If the player collides with an object, life depletes, until there are no more lives. The player has 5 lives by default, and increases by one for every 10,000 points reached. Score for the longest time!
