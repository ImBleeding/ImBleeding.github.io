About Funny Shooter 2

Funny Shooter 2 is a fun FPS game where you fight hordes of absurd enemies! Use various weapons to slay these bizarre beings and upgrade your weapons arsenal as you progress. Immerse yourself in this fun world!

How to play Funny Shooter 2 online?

Mouse - look around WASD - movement W + Shift - run Space - jump Left Mouse Button - shoot Right Mouse Button (Hold) - aim Mouse wheel - next/leading weapon 1-7 - weapon hotkeys R - reload G - throw a grenade T - inspect the weapon E - remove / remove weapon
