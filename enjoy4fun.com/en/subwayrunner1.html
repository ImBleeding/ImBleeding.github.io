About Subway Runner 1

Enter the railway! Run as far as you possibly can, evade obstacles of the railway and finish all the achievements in an everchanging and gradually more difficult level design! Collect coins and use them to upgrade your collectible power up capabilities. Challenge your skills and beat your highscore!

How to play Subway Runner 1 online?

Run on the railway and evade obstacles by jumping and sliding to get as far as possible. Collect coins to purchase upgrades, making your powerups more potent. Collect DASH MASTER slogan every run, finish achievements along the way and prove you are the best runner out there!
