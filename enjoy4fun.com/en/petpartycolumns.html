About Pet Party Columns

Pet Party Columns is a Tetris-like puzzle game where you direct 3-piece blocks in order to match three or more of the identical symbols. Pay attention to the arrangement of the animals in the grids, as misplacing the blocks consecutively will cause you the round. Can you position all the animals in the right grids and complete the game?

How to play Pet Party Columns online?

Shift the position of falling blocks to left or right. The game ends when blocks fill all the grid rows completely. Shift - Left / Right arrow keys Accelerate - Down arrow key
