About Hop Ball

Come on! Test your reflexes and hop skills! Hop Ball is a ball game that lets ball hop on the tiles until the end of the color road. This game will create a whole new experience for ball game fans.

How to play Hop Ball online?

Swipe the screen or move the mouse to jump balls on tile's color, hop ball on them and control carefully! Keep calm and go as far as possible in this ball game!
