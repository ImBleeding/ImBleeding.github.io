About RollBall

A super fast-paced game, traversing countless unpredictable obstacles, time is running out! Roll, spin and don't hit the obstacles! It's a challenging course in an abstract world, but with uncompromising physics! You can easily control the RollBall with just one finger, but you may not be able to master it completely! Only speed and concentration can help you unlock more levels and skins, come and meet the challenge! A lot of unique and rare skins are waiting for you to unlock! Featured: - Simple one-finger swipe to control the ball - Collect many different rare balls - Vivid 3D graphics
