About Join Blocks 2048

Play Join Blocks puzzle today and every day - it's the best, free 2048 classic number puzzle game! Escape to the most relaxing 2048 number blocks merging game around! Tap, shoot and join x2 blocks up to 2048 and more.

How to play Join Blocks 2048 online?

Click the number to make the number +1. When the three adjacent numbers are the same, it can be eliminated. Continuous elimination will trigger the combo effect.
