About Frostwing

Frostwing is a puzzle platformer created by Diemorth. Help our little penguin friend with his adventures as it works its way through challenging winter-themed levels to collect some frozen feathers. Tap or click on glowing orange blocks to slide them. That way, you can jump on these blocks and use them as a pedestal to jump onto higher places. Can you collect every feather in the game and help our penguin be the most beautiful animal in town?

How to play Frostwing online?

Tap on an orange block to slide it. Move - WASD or Arrow keys Jump - Space bar, W or Up
