About Transporter

Deliver the highly radioactive Fancadium Ore to the Storage. - nmskr. Made with Drive Mad Kit.

How to play Transporter online?

The player must move this cart all the way to the end of the level, all while balancing decently enough to make the ball remain on the cart. At the end of a level, the player must deliver the ball to the storage. If either the car gets destroyed or the ball gets out of the cart, the player would lose the level.
