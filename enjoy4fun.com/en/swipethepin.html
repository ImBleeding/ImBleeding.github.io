Swipe The Pin

About Swipe The Pin

Swipe The Pin is a fun and challenging game. The game fills the glass container with the colored balls that are blocked by the pins. Touch on the pin to open the path of the ball, and put the ball into the glass case. When the container is full / there are no balls left, a complete level popup will appear. Tap to go to the next level.

How to play Swipe The Pin online?

Touch on the pin.
