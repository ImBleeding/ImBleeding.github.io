About Square Jet

Square Jet is a minimalist platformer game that is pretty addicting and fun to play. Help the little guy to move on the platform and pass through each level fill with various dangerous obstacles by using his jetpack to fly up. The level increases in difficulty as the level progress and makes it challenging.

How to play Square Jet online?

Use left/right arrow to move, press up arrow to jump and press again and hold to fly.
