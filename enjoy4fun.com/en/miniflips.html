Mini Flips

About Mini Flips

Mini Flips is an arcade game to collect all the coins in the arena by jumping, flipping sides, and avoiding obstacles. Complete over 160 levels in this challenging game!

How to play Mini Flips online?

Desktop Controls: C / up arrow key / left-click = jump Space / right-click = flip side while on the arrow panels Mobile Controls: Tap the right side of the screen = jump Tap the left side of the screen = flip side while on the arrow panels
