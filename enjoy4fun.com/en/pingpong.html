About ping pong

Ping Pong is a sports game created by MarketJS. Grab one of the digital paddles and enjoy an exciting Ping Pong experience. In this simple yet challenging sports game, you need to hit the ball to your opponent's side of the table. First player to reach 10 wins the match! So be quick on your feet and confuse your opponent with tricky shoots. You can even control the speed of your hits by paying attention to the intensity of your flick. Go ahead and beat the clever AI opponents this game has!
