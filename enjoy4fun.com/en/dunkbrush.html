About Dunkbrush

Dunkbrush is a basketball skill game where you have to draw lines to make the ball fall into the basket. Make sure you spot the trajectory of the ball quickly, and draw the line that makes sure the ball goes in. Gather coins to unlock new drawing colours. Can you set the high score?

How to play Dunkbrush online?

Click and drag your mouse to create a line.
