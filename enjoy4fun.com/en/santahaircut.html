About Santa Haircut

Santa Haircut is a funny haircut game featuring Santa! Santa is getting ready for Christmas night, so he goes to your salon to get a makeover. Let's help him cut out his messy hair and give the best hair makeover he can get. You can dress him up at the end to perfect his new look! If you like this game, you should try to style a regular customer's hair.

How to play Santa Haircut online?

Use the mouse to click and use items.
