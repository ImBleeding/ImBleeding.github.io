About Mahjong Pyramids

Mahjong Pyramids is a puzzle game created by MarketJS. In this game, your objective is to match ancient pyramid mahjong tiles and to complete levels. Start with the tutorial level to learn the ropes when it comes to destroying matching tiles. Move on to exploring various power-ups and bonuses to help you along the way. Immerse yourself in a cool Egyptian-themed mahjong experience like no other!

How to play Mahjong Pyramids online?

Use your finger or the left mouse button to select a tile.
