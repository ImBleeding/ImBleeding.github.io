About Gummy Blocks Battle

If you are a fan of Gummy Blocks you'll surely love its Multiplayer version! Challenge real players all over the world! Clear two or more lines to launch locks that will block your opponent! Clear lines to get extra time. Are you ready? May the best win!

How to play Gummy Blocks Battle online?

Create or destroy full lines on the screen, both vertical and horizontal. Clear two or more lines to launch locks to your opponent. Clear lines to get extra time.
