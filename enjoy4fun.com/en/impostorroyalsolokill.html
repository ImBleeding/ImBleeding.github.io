About Impostor Royal Solo Kill

Prepare for a breathtaking Impostor battle. You have to be fast and agile in order to be the last survivor in the game that takes place in the space shuttle. Challenge other Impostors by getting some bonuses and weapons with the drops falling on certain areas with the parachute. Fight to be the last man on the ship by killing your enemies with a sword, gun or chainsaw! Customize your character with 4 different levels, 12 different Impostors and 15 different hats. You can make these customizations with the points you earn from the levels.
