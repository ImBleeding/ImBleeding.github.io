About Run

The game slowly corrupting and you better run as far as you can!

How to play Run online?

Tap the left or right arrows to move left or right. Tap the up arrow to jump. You need to move away to the right as quickly as possible to avoid the game corruption catching up to you. Death is caused by succumbing to the corrupted background, or by falling into water. Note that you cannot jump off of certain surfaces, such as the thin wooden beams, the edges of platforms, and the edges of a tree. As the game progresses, you are met with a greater variation of terrain, and you must thus more readily adapt to it. You can also gain coins along the way.
