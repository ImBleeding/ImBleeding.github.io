Power Boxes

About Power Boxes

Swipe to control. Move the boxes to power up all the light bulbs. Too much power may cause a power surge, so be careful!

How to play Power Boxes online?

Swipe to move boxes. There are 3 types of boxes: Power boxes which create electricity and can light up bulbs (it can only conduct electricity when struck by a power surge), Metal boxes which conduct electricity from power boxes or other adjacent metal boxes, and Wooden boxes which cannot conduct electricity. To win, light up all bulbs
