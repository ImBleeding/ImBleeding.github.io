About Basketball Kings

Four unique locations where you can play street basketball are waiting for you. Start playing, shoot the basket and repeat. Reach a high score to unlock new balls. Which ball will you play best with?

How to play Basketball Kings online?

A simple basketball game where your goal is to shoot the basket as many times as you can within the time limit. You have to be quick and precise. Try to reach the highest score and new balls will unlock. The longer you play, the less place for mistake you have.
