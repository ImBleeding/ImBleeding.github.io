About Basket Ball

Basket & Ball is a basketball game like no other. Combining the sports and the puzzle genres, the game forces you to figure out creative ways to get the ball into the basket! Your job is to bounce the basketball and score passing through a wide variety of physics puzzles. Gather plenty of bonuses before you sink the shot in Basket & Ball! How high can you score?

How to play Basket Ball online?

Bounce ball - Left mouse button or Space Move - Arrow keys
