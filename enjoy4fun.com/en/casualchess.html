About Casual Chess

Casual Chess is a card game created by MarketJS. Enjoy a classic chess match against the computer. There are three different themes, three difficulty settings, and even a hint system to guide your moves. Use every tool given to you to outwit your opponent. Checkmate!

How to play Casual Chess online?

Click or tap on a piece to select it. Then tap on an empty tile to move your selected piece.
