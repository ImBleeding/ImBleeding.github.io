About Power Badminton

Power Badminton is a single player sports game, where the player must earn more points than his opponent to win the match. Choose a character, hop on the court and demonstrate your bandmintong skills by defeating all of the other contestants!

How to play Power Badminton online?

Move back and forth and hit the shuttlecock (the ball) to get it into your opponent's area and score points. Move - A (Left) and D (Right) Hit - J (High), K (Low) and L (Close hit)
