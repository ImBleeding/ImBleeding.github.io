Braindom 2: Who is Lying

About Braindom 2: Who is Lying

Braindom 2: Who is Lying is a brain game filled with tricky puzzles and mind games to stimulate your intellect. Become a master of logical deduction and solve the various mysteries presented to you in each level.

How to play Braindom 2: Who is Lying online?

Investigate the claim Each level features a unique scenario where you must determine what is true. Sometimes you are to figure out which person is lying, other times you may simply be investigating who is who. Check the evidence There is a chance to investigate the evidence in all of the puzzling scenarios. You’ll find little clues hidden around each scene. Use this information to find the correct answer! Select the right answer After scoping out the scene for various bits of intel, pick the answer you think is correct to progress up the path to new stages and milestones! There are 150 levels to explore and new characters to meet.
