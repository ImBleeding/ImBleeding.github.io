About Icy Purple Head 2

Icy Purple Head 2 is a puzzle game created by Mini Duck Games. Help the purple head reach the end of each level! The purple head cannot move by itself, so you have to help by switching between purple sticky mode and slippy icy mode. It's all about timing! Each level contains objects and obstacles to help you move around. Watch out for the traps on the way!

How to play Icy Purple Head 2 online?

Press or release mouseclick to switch between modes
