About Truck Loader

Truck Loader is a classic puzzle game where you have to load cargo onto a truck with your forklift. Boxes are spread across levels, and you have to collect them and load up the truck to complete the level. Some levels are easy and you are just driving around to collect the boxes, but as you proceed in the game the levels become harder. Perform special moves or follow sequences to solve the puzzle and complete the level. You can now play the Truck Loader series on your desktop or mobile device for free on enjoy4fun!

How to play Truck Loader online?

Move left/right - A/D Jump - W Pick up boxes - Point and click with your mouse
