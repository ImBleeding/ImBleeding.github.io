About Bridge of Doom

The dwarven hero enters the forgotten depths equipped with a powerful runic hammer and an ancient shield. You must fight the goblins and avoid deadly traps along the way. The platforms behind you are constantly collapsing, so you must move quickly! A simple but engaging clicker Endless gameplay Monsters and traps Race against the time.

How to play Bridge of Doom online?

Simply click to move your hero - each click moves the hero one space forward. To destroy the goblins, you must click rapidly - be careful though if you click too much, you could fall on a trap! Time your moves carefully but move through the deadly bridge of doom as quickly as you can!
