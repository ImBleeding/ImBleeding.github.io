About Mika Candy Adventure

Mika's Candy Adventure is a casual puzzle game created by Fumingo. Your objective is to push cupcakes around and to get them to their correct locations. Once all the cupcakes are in their right spot, you will be able to advance to the next level. You can use the undo power-up to revert your moves, rethink and play again. This is the most delicious puzzle game ever made. Can you help Mika save all the cupcakes?

How to play Mika Candy Adventure online?

Move - WASD / Arrow keys Zoom - Mouse wheel / Double swipe
