How to play Unblock Puzzle online?

Challenging puzzle game for people of all ages. Simply unblock the red block out of the board by sliding the other blocks out of the way. Create an optimal path with minimal moves. If there is a difficult level, you can use the hint. The game comes with 4 difficulties ranging from Beginner to Expert. Enjoy hours of gameplay with 240 levels and keep your mind sharp.
