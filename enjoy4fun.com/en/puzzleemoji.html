About Puzzle Emoji

Emoji Puzzle is a puzzle game that requires you to associate similar feelings in pairs. Consider each problem and identify its theme. Simply tap each piece from a separate column one at a time to link them with a line. Alternatively, you may drag to link objects from various columns with a line. You complete the level if you successfully connect every piece.
