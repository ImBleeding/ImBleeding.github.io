About Mahjong

Mahjohng is a tile-based game commonly played by four players with a set of 144 tiles based on Chinese characters and symbols.

How to play Mahjong online?

Match tiles to solve classic Mahjong puzzles. You can only remove uncovered tiles which are free on at least one side. Scour each board layout for matching letters, numbers, and shapes. Since there is no time limit, you can play at your own pace.
