About Monster Mash

Monster Mash is an idle clicker game where you merge monster babies into stronger tiers to have the ultimate monster roster! You will earn gold and unlock new monsters by advancing organically in the game as well as completing missions. Unlock monsters of the highest tier and brag to your friends! (And don’t forget to come back every day to collect your offline loot.)

How to play Monster Mash online?

Merge monsters - Left mouse button
