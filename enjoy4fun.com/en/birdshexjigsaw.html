About Birds Hex Jigsaw

Birds Hex Jigsaw is a different flavor of classical jigsaw game. Arrange the hex pieces of a picture to form the original picture. There are 2 modes in the games. Each mode has 24 levels. You must complete a level within time limits.

How to play Birds Hex Jigsaw online?

Use any pointing (mouse, touchpad etc) device to play this game.
