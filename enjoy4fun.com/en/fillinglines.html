About Filling Lines

Filling Lines is a New HTML5 Puzzle Game. In this connection game, you should connect each shape to its similar, in order to fill the puzzle in the correct order. If you do not connect the shapes in the correct order, you will not win the level.

How to play Filling Lines online?

Click and drag the line to connect the tiles.
