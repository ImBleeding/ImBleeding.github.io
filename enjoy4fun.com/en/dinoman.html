About Dinoman

Swipe to move, collect all the coins, and avoid the ghosts!

How to play Dinoman online?

A yellow dinosaur, must navigate through a maze and collect all the yellow dots, whilst avoiding 4 other dinosaurs (pink, blue, red, orange). You have three lives, and each life is lost by colliding with another dinosaur. Once all yellow dots (worth 10 score) are collected, the level will reset, and a new round begins. For each subsequent level, the dinosaurs move faster, up until a point where they will outrun you. This increases the difficulty of the game as you play on. You do not gain score by completing a level.
