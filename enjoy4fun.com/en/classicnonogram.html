About Classic Nonogram

If you love Sudoku, then you will definitely like this new puzzle game! The goal of the game is to determine which cells need to be painted over and which ones should be left empty. The numbers in the rows and columns will give you a clue, but be careful. There are only three lives!

How to play Classic Nonogram online?

Tap the cell to fill it
