About rabbit-samurai-2

Rabbit Samurai 2 is a platform game where you rescue your bunny friends trapped in a mysterious forest. Follow the trails of carrot and pick up each and every one. Whenever you see a caged rabbit, reach it and set it free by passing through various obstacles such as mazes, tree trunks, springs and bouncy platforms. Use your grappling hook and your fast reflexes to launch yourself up using your ninja skills. The second instalment is much more dangerous and rewarding than the first Rabbit Samurai!
