About Forgotten Treasure 2

Forgotten Treasure 2 is a puzzle game where you combine jewels to crash obstacles using your logic and go through numerous challenges. At the moment, there are more than a thousand levels and this is far from the limit. Some of them are real tests for the brain!

How to play Forgotten Treasure 2 online?

Click or drag the left mouse button to move, combine and match jewels in a row.
