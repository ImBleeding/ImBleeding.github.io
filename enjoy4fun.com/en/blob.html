About Blob

Help this purple slime escape from the lab!

How to play Blob online?

There are 25 levels present. You have the option to either move left or right or jump. You must avoid the green slime at the bottom; this triggers game over. The end of the level is shown by a metallic tunnel out of the which the blob can escape. The blob is rather heavy, and may thus slide off of a platform if most of its mass is sticking out. It is also very malleable and thus it can shapeshift into tight spaces to a certain degree. The game features puzzle solving to a certain extent; for example you may have to trigger buttons/levers and use boxes to climb onto certain platforms.
