About Super Peaman World

Would you like to go on adventures in different worlds with Super Peaman? Collect peanuts, gold and fight enemies. Throw peanuts at enemies or jump on their heads to destroy them. Achieve victory by completing 32 levels in 3 different Worlds.

How to play Super Peaman World online?
