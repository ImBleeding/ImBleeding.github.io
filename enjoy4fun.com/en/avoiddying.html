About Avoid Dying

Avoid Dying is an archery game where you must use your aiming skills to hit the target with an arrow. Your score increases with each successful hit, but missing the target may have dire consequences like a giant booby trap falling over you and crushing you. Share the game with your friends and see who can score the highest in this addictive bow and arrow shooter!

How to play Avoid Dying online?

Hold down the left mouse button and aim using your cursor.
