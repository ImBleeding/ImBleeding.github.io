Tower Boom

About Tower Boom

Tower Boom is a puzzle physics-based demolition game, your mission is to destroy all towers by placing dynamites. Enjoy the game!

How to play Tower Boom online?

Click anywhere on the screen to pick the dynamite, place it on the concrete, press detonator to explode all dynamites. Win each level by taking the rubble down under the limit line.
