About Two Cups

Your girlfriend, Cupgirl, has been smashed by a gigantic nasty cat who loves to break cups along with other stuff... The Cupgirl pieces are lost in countryside and it is up to you to fight back the nasty cat, collect all cup fragments, put your girlfriend back togheter, and live happily ever after with her.

How to play Two Cups online?

A or left arrow key = move left . D or right arrow key = move right . W or Z or up arrow key = jump .
