About Inversion 2048

A 2048 game, but with a twist. The world inverts itself. Along with the world, all the blocks invert themselves, and get a secondary number. Got stuck? Look into the in-game shop, where you can spend your earned points! You can also buy some skins, but lookout for scams in the shop... well, it's in-game points anyway. Sit down relax and enjoy INVERSION 2048.

How to play Inversion 2048 online?

USE ARROW KEYS OR MOUSE OR TOUCH TO NAVIGATE AND PLAY!
