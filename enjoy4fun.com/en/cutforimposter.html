About Cut For Imposter

An arcade game about the adventures of a character named Imposter, who constantly wants to have a delicious meal. He has only one desire - to eat sweets suspended over his head. Feed the Imposter by cutting the ropes holding the candy, this will help send it straight into his mouth.

How to play Cut For Imposter online?

Smartphone, tablet - touch control Computer - mouse control
