About M Game

One tap 2D golf game

How to play M Game online?

There is a ball the player can aim and shoot, and two cups: one acts as a platform and the other as the hole to shoot for. Once the ball is static, a line of trajectory with limited range is shown while aiming. The player can tap the screen to shoot the ball in the trajectory. A point is scored every time a ball shoots to the cup. The game ends once the ball falls beyond the cups. The ball may shoot an unlimited number of times before scoring or losing.
