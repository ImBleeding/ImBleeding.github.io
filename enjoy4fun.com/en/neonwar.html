About Neon War

Neon War is a skill game where you control a cannon that shoots its balls periodically into the air. Hold down the left mouse button to take control of the cannon and slide it horizontally to control its position. Remember that the cannon shoots by itself, so all you have to do is keep it in the right place while aiming for the blocks with numbers on them. The numbers represent how many times you have to hit the object. As you progress, you will be able to unlock bonus upgrades to features such as damage, fire rate, health, or pet. Go ahead and give it Neon War a try!
